Miso Soup

Description

Miso soup is a Japanese staple. It can be enjoyed at any time of the day, but it is commonly served at breakfast time. It is super simple to make and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp dashi powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tbsp miso paste
  • 8 oz tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, diced

Steps

  1. Bring a medium pot with 4 cups of water and dashi powder to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat to medium. Dissolve miso paste into the broth by whisking it on a ladle or large spoon.
  3. Once the miso paste has dissolved into the broth, add the tofu. Gently stir so as to not break the tofu.
  4. Add green onion. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
